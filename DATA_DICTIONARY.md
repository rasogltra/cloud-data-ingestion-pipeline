# Data Dictionary - Ads Spend
**Ad Spend Field Name Descriptions** \
**date** - date of the record \
**client** - client name \
**platform** - Ad platform (Google Ads, TikTok, etc.) \
**campaign_id** - (Unique Campaign ID) \
**campaign_name** \
**channel** - Marketing Channel \
**spend_usd** - Total spend in USD \
**impressions** - No. of times ad was shown \
**clicks** - No. of times ad clicks \
**conversions** - No. of sucessful outcomes
